About Us

Digital Innovation Hub Timor (DIHT) is a platform empowering Timor-Leste youth to innovate, collaborate, and create sustainable digital solutions.

We believe in the power of technology to transform communities and create lasting impact. Through our programs and initiatives, we're building a vibrant ecosystem where young innovators can thrive.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading digital innovation hub in Timor-Leste, empowering youth to create impactful solutions.

Our Mission

  • Empower youth through digital learning and innovation.
  • Create collaborative spaces for students, professionals, and communities.
  • Promote projects with social, educational, and environmental impact.
  • Connect Timor-Leste with global innovation networks.
